Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel during your billing period, you'll continue to have access until the end of that period. We don't charge cancellation fees.

Do you offer discounts for families?

Yes! We offer a 15% family discount when you enroll 3 or more family members. Contact our support team for more information about setting up a family plan.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our service within the first 14 days, we'll refund your payment in full, no questions asked.

Are the teachers qualified?

All our teachers are highly qualified with Ijazah certification and years of teaching experience. They undergo rigorous screening and continuous training to ensure the highest quality of Quran education.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remaining days in your billing cycle. Downgrades will take effect at your next billing date.

Still have questions?

Our support team is ready to help you choose the right plan for your Quran learning journey.
